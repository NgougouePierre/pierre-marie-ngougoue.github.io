Research

I work on the mathematical analysis of fluid–structure interactions (FSI) and related PDE systems. A current focus is barotropic compressible fluid–shell interactions and local well-posedness questions.

Model equations (MathJax)

Compressible Navier–Stokes (barotropic):

$$\begin{aligned} &\partial_t \rho + \nabla\!\cdot(\rho u)=0,\\ &\partial_t(\rho u) + \nabla\!\cdot(\rho u\otimes u) + \nabla p(\rho) = \mu \Delta u + (\lambda+\mu)\nabla(\nabla\!\cdot u), \end{aligned}$$ with $p=p(\rho)$ barotropic, viscosity coefficients $\mu>0$, $\lambda+\tfrac{2}{3}\mu\ge0$.

Elastic shell (linear Koiter-type, schematic):

$$\rho_s\, \partial_{tt} \eta + \mathcal{K}\eta + \mathcal{D}\partial_t\eta = \big(\sigma(u,\rho)\, n\big)\!\cdot n \;\;\text{on }\Gamma, $$ where $\eta$ is the shell displacement, $\mathcal{K}$ the elastic operator, $\mathcal{D}$ damping, and the right-hand side is the normal fluid traction on the interface $\Gamma$.
